In architecture, a pendentive is the structural innovation that allows a circular dome to rest on a square base—a smooth transition between incompatible geometries.

In biotech R&D, laboratory methods, data systems, manufacturing processes, and AI models each have their own logic. Making them work together requires thoughtful integration.

Services

AI and Data Strategy

Identifying where AI and data infrastructure genuinely add value in R&D—and where they don't. We help organizations avoid hype, evaluate vendor claims critically, and build roadmaps aligned to scientific and business goals.

Integration Architecture

Connecting laboratory systems, data platforms, manufacturing processes, and analytical tools in ways that reduce friction and preserve information. Using frameworks like Domain-Driven Design to align technical architecture with how science actually gets done.

Technical Due Diligence

Evaluating technology investments, vendor solutions, and internal proposals for scientific plausibility, implementation risk, and genuine usefulness. Particularly relevant for AI, automation, and data infrastructure in life sciences and adjacent fields.

Fractional Leadership

Interim and fractional executive roles for organizations navigating complex technology transitions. Engagement-based leadership for initiatives spanning science, technology, and operations.
