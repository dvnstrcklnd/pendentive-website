Integrative system architecture for complex technology projects

In architecture, a pendentive is a smooth structural transition between a square space and a circular dome—a simple, elegant innovation that joins disparate elements into a unified whole.

Today's R&D depends on bringing together laboratory methods, manufacturing processes, data systems, and AI models. Even when these parts work individually, integrating them creates friction, instability, and information loss.

We provide strategic leadership to solve these problems.
